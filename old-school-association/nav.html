WELCOME

FEDERAL GOVERNMENT COLLEGE VANDEIKYA OLD STUDENTS ASSOCIATION, FEDVOSA aims at promoting unity amongst old students while fulfilling our responsibility of harnessing and maximizing our resources and potentials to the general welfare, peace, and progress of our members worldwide.

History of FEDVOSA

The FGC Vandeikya Old Students Association came into existence in 2010, ten (10) years after the first graduates were turned out of the college that was established in 1994. The idea of the Old Students Association was conceived when it was considered necessary to create an organization that would embrace different generations of the school. The association plays a dominant role in the life of the school and old Students by promote social interaction among ourselves and contributing to the welfare and academic development of our Alma-mater, Federal Government College Vandeikya. Over the years, a lot of positive developments have been recorded in the management and administration of the Association.

Aims and Objectives

  • To create a forum for all the members of FGC Vandeikya Old Students Association to come together for the purpose of sharing their ideas and efforts towards the progress of members of the association in particular and FGC Vandeikya in general.
  • To encourage and promote equality, unity, peace, harmony and progress among members.
  • To offer aid or assistance to any member of the association where and when necessary.
  • To perform or undertake any assignment(s) functions(s) or do any other act in furtherance of the interest of the association.
  • The association is non-political and non-religious and shall be open to all graduates of Federal Government College Vandeikya, Benue State.
